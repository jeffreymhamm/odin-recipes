Jeff's Lasagna

Description

This is the lasagna that I am using for the test page, no clue if it is any good or not.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb ground pork
  • 1/2 lb ground been
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 28 oz can of crushed tomatoes
  • 8 oz can of tomato sauce
  • 2 tbs chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1.5 tsp dried basil
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • .5 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/8 tsp white sugar
  • 16 oz lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb cottage cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 16 oz shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Cook pork and beef in large skillet
  3. Add onion and cook until translucent
  4. Stir in crushed tomato, tomato sauce and all spices
  5. While sauce is simmering, heat a large pot of water and cook the pasta, preheat oven to 375
  6. In a bowl, mix cottage cheese, Parmesan Cheese, Eggs, and remaining pasley, salt and pepper until combined
  7. Assemble Lasagna and bake for 40 minutes
  8. Let stand for 10 minutes, serve and enjoy!!
